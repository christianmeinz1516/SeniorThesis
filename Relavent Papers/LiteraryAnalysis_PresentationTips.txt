PRESENTATION -- TIPS

1. Start with Questions to answer or what I am researching
2. Provide necessary information going into research (with source)
   (Discuss background necessary for audience)
3. Describe the methods of solving/exploring the problem
4. Past research results, what has been said so far.
   (Explain how results relate to why I am extending to my research)
5. Note any important aspects of the project that needs particular 
   attention.
6. Re-iterate my proposal (what I am finding/hypothesizing).
7. "Thank you for listening. Any questions?"

PREPARE A THESIS STATEMENT - if can be

* Provide Figures to help support frameworks and algorithms
* Think about the audience. They might not know security and crypto
